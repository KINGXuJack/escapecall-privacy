Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

Thank you for using EscapeCall. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

EscapeCall is a local fake call simulator designed to help you gracefully exit awkward social situations. We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • We do not collect any personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • We do not collect any usage data or analytics
  • We do not collect any device information or identifiers
  • We do not use any tracking technologies (cookies, pixels, SDKs)
  • We do not require any account registration or login
  • We do not share any data with third parties

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app may store the following information only on your device:

  • Call History: Records of simulated calls you create within the app are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.
  • App Settings: Your preferences (such as selected ringtone, call timing, and contact names for simulations) are stored locally on your device.

All locally stored data can be deleted at any time by deleting the app from your device.

3. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions on your device:

  • Notifications: We may request permission to send local notifications to simulate incoming calls at scheduled times. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any server communication.

You can manage or revoke these permissions at any time through your device's Settings app.

4. Third-Party Services

EscapeCall does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data collection tools. The app functions entirely offline on your device.

5. Data Security

Since all data related to your use of the app is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted to external servers, your information remains under your complete control. We rely on Apple's built-in iOS security features to protect data stored on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed to function locally on your device without collecting any data, regardless of the user's age.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: All data generated by the app is stored on your device and accessible through the app.
  • Right to Delete: You can delete all app data by removing the app from your device.
  • Right to Control: You can manage app permissions through your device's Settings at any time.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.
